Our Parties

On the first Saturday of every month we hold a "CossiePlay Party" at the town hall. Parties start at 6pm and run until 11 pm. There will always be a dj who takes requests and dancing is common. You can register to attend these parties by signing up.

Entry Prices

  • Adults - $15
  • Ages 6-17 - $10
  • Kids under 6 - Free
Each entry includes one non-alcoholic drink and a glowstick.

Party Guidelines

  • Our party-goers attend our parties at their own risk and are responsible for their own safety. Check with your doctor before partying hard
  • Party Fees must be paid at the door. Cash or EFTPOS accepted.Credit card payments attract a 2% surcharge.
  • Alcohol is served at the bar to over 18's only.
  • No intoxicated person will be served but will be escorted to a taxi - paid by the party-goer
